Pig Shots (Oven Baked)

Pig Shots Recipe

Pig shots are a great appetizer for any event. They are delicious and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Smoked sausage or kielbasa sausage
  • Thick-cut bacon
  • Cream cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Jalapeno
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Slap Ya Mama seasoning
  • BBQ sauce
  • Honey
  • Brown sugar
  • Jalapeno slices

Steps

  1. Make the sauce. Mix the BBQ sauce, honey, and brown sugar together in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Make the cups. Cut the bacon in half. Take a half of a slice of bacon and wrap the bacon around the smoke sausages. Then, secure them with a toothpick or place them in a muffin tin.
  3. Make the cream cheese mixture. Mix the cream cheese, sour cream, diced jalapeno, sharp cheddar, and seasonings together in a medium bowl.
  4. Add the mixture to the sausage cups. Add the cream cheese mixture to a piping bag or a quart-size baggie and clip the bottom corner. Then, squeeze the cream cheese filling into each cup.
  5. Bake. Top each shot with a sliced jalapeno and then brush each one with the honey BBQ mixture. Bake at 375 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes or until the bacon is crispy.
  6. Enjoy. Carefully remove the shots from the muffin tin and enjoy!
